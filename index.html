// the problem

AI agents are becoming useful. Trust is the bottleneck.

Coding agents and browser automation tools can now do real work. But most people do not want those systems operating directly on their real laptop, browser profile, or sensitive files.

02

People want browser agents, but they do not want them using their real browser profile, session state, or personal accounts casually.

03

Capability without control is a bad trade
